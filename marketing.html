How it works

From photo to outfit in three steps.

No manual sorting, no endless tagging. FitsAI does the heavy lifting so you can focus on getting dressed.

01

Photograph your clothes

Take a photo of any garment. FitsAI removes the background automatically and uses AI to tag the category, colour, and style — no input needed from you.

02

Set your vibe

Choose an occasion (work, casual, date night…), a mood, and the weather. FitsAI uses your selections as the brief for the AI stylist.

03

Swipe, save, wear

Browse AI-generated outfit cards, swipe right to save the ones you love, and build a personal lookbook from your own wardrobe.
